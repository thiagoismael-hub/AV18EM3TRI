2.1. According to the text, why does the smile of the Mona Lisa seem different every time we look at it?
(Suggested Answer): Because the emotions she is expressing are difficult to understand, which makes her smile seem slightly different every time.
2.2. Why do you think Leonardo da Vinci's curiosity helped him become a genius? Give examples from the text.
(Suggested Answer - answers will vary):
His curiosity pushed him to study many different subjects and make connections between them. This is how he "taught himself to be a genius."
Examples from the text:
  • He dissected human faces to understand how muscles and nerves create expressions (which he used in the Mona Lisa).
  • He created mechanical props (like flying machines) for theatre, which led him to design real flying machines.
  • He was curious about everyday things, asking questions like "Why is the sky blue?" or "Describe the tongue of the woodpecker."
